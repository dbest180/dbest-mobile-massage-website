Booking & Scheduling

How do I book a mobile massage session?

You can book a session through our online contact form, by phone, or by email. We recommend using the contact form for the fastest response. After submitting your request, we'll contact you within 24 hours to confirm availability and finalize your appointment.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance for weekday appointments and 5-7 days for weekend appointments. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

What are your service hours?

We offer appointments:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Saturday - Sunday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Evening and weekend appointments are popular, so we recommend booking these well in advance.

Do you service my area?

We serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. There are no travel fees within our standard service area. For locations outside this area, a small travel fee may apply. Please provide your complete address when booking, and we'll confirm if your location is within our service area.

Can I book same-day appointments?

Yes, we accept same-day appointments based on availability. Please call or text us directly for same-day booking requests. A $25 same-day booking fee may apply for appointments requested less than 4 hours in advance.

Preparation & What to Expect

What do I need to prepare for my massage?

Very little! We bring everything needed. Just prepare:

  • A quiet space large enough for the massage table (about 6x8 feet)
  • Access to an electrical outlet
  • Comfortable room temperature
  • Minimal background noise

We'll handle setup, cleanup, and bring all equipment including the table, linens, oils, and music.

What should I wear during the massage?

Most clients undress completely, but you should undress only to your level of comfort. You will be properly draped with sheets and blankets at all times. Some clients prefer to keep underwear on, which is perfectly fine.

For sports massage: Athletic wear may be appropriate. We'll discuss this during your consultation.

Should I shower before my session?

It's recommended but not required. A quick shower helps relax muscles and removes lotions or oils that might interfere with massage oils. However, if you're coming straight from work or exercise, we completely understand!

Should I eat before my massage?

It's best to avoid heavy meals 1-2 hours before your session. A light snack is fine. Being overly full or hungry can be distracting during your massage.

What if I'm sick?

If you have a fever, contagious illness, or are generally unwell, please reschedule your appointment. Massage can sometimes worsen illness and puts both of us at risk. There's no penalty for illness-related cancellations with advance notice.

During Your Session

What happens during my first session?

Your first session includes:

  1. Consultation: We'll discuss your health history, concerns, and goals (10-15 minutes)
  2. Setup: I'll set up the massage table and equipment while you prepare
  3. Massage: Your chosen service with ongoing check-ins about pressure
  4. Aftercare: Recommendations and follow-up discussion

Please allow extra 15-20 minutes for your first session for the consultation.

What if the pressure is too deep or not deep enough?

Please speak up! Communication is essential for an effective massage. You can say:

  • "Could you use a little less pressure here?"
  • "This area needs more pressure"
  • "That's perfect pressure"

I'll also check in periodically about pressure and comfort. There's no need to suffer in silence - your feedback helps me provide the best care.

Is it normal to fall asleep during massage?

Absolutely! Many clients doze off during their sessions, which is a sign that your body is relaxing deeply. Don't worry - it's completely normal and actually quite common.

What if I need to use the bathroom during the session?

No problem at all! Just let me know, and I'll step out of the room so you can use the bathroom. We can pause the timer and resume when you return.

Can I talk during the massage?

It's your session! Some clients prefer quiet relaxation, while others enjoy conversation. I follow your lead. If you prefer silence, that's perfectly fine. If you have questions or want to chat, I'm happy to engage.

Policies & Procedures

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the time to other clients.

24+ hours notice: No charge, full rescheduling flexibility
4-24 hours notice: 50% of service fee
Less than 4 hours notice: Full service fee

Emergency situations are considered case by case.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept:

  • Cash
  • All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Venmo
  • Zelle
  • Apple Pay

Payment is collected at the time of service unless other arrangements are made in advance.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer refunds for services rendered. If you're unsatisfied with your massage, please let us know immediately so we can address your concerns during the session. We're committed to your satisfaction and will make adjustments as needed.

For package purchases, unused sessions may be refunded minus a 10% administrative fee.

What is your late arrival policy?

If you're running late, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll do our best to provide your full session, but if the delay impacts subsequent appointments, your session may need to be shortened with full payment due.

Arrivals more than 15 minutes late without notice may be treated as a cancellation.

What is your code of conduct?

We maintain the highest professional standards:

  • Proper draping at all times
  • Professional boundaries and conduct
  • Respect for client privacy and modesty
  • Zero tolerance for inappropriate behavior

We reserve the right to terminate any session immediately if these standards are not respected, with full payment due.

General Information

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We are fully licensed by the state (#LMT12345) and carry professional liability insurance. We can provide documentation upon request.

Do you offer couples massage or group sessions?

Currently, we focus on individual sessions to provide the highest quality care. For couples or group sessions, we can schedule back-to-back appointments, but each person receives an individual session.

Can I purchase gift certificates?

Yes! We offer digital gift certificates that can be customized for any service or dollar amount. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, holidays, or just because. Contact us to purchase a gift certificate.

Do you work with clients who have medical conditions?

Yes, with proper communication. Please disclose all medical conditions during booking. We may require doctor's clearance for certain conditions like:

  • Recent surgery or injury
  • Blood clots or circulation issues
  • Pregnancy (congratulations!)
  • Cancer or cancer treatment
  • Autoimmune conditions

We'll adapt techniques to ensure your safety and comfort.

How often should I get a massage?

This depends on your goals:

  • Stress management: Every 2-4 weeks
  • Chronic pain: Weekly or bi-weekly initially, then maintenance
  • Injury recovery: As recommended by your healthcare provider
  • General wellness: Monthly
  • Athletic performance: 1-2 times per month

We can help you determine the ideal frequency during your session.
